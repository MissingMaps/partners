    Hosting A Mapathon

     

    Before the event

    • Choose a date and time for your event. We recommend a 90 minute to 2 hour event as a best practice
    • Set up a page for your event on Benevity, the Employee Giving Portal. You may need to talk to the Volunteer Champion at your location for this.
    • Send out a Calendar invite in advance with Virtual Conference as the location (which will generate WebEx call-in information) or use whatever videoconferencing tool your team is used to.
    • You'll need a facilitator or two to train mappers and lead the event.
      •        ◦ If you have not participated in Missing Maps before or haven’t organized a mapathon before, the organizer or event leader should watch the tutorial videos above to get a sense of how it works so you can answer questions and make sure mappers are contributing the right way. Then try mapping for 30 minutes to an hour to get a hang of it.
             ◦ If more than one person can watch the tutorial videos and do some mapping before the event, that’s even better! More people to help out.
    • Send a reminder 1-2 days before your event, reminding attendees to register a mapping user ID in advance
    • Pick a task from the AMM Missing Maps page. The Red Cross asked us for help with these projects, which are all good for beginners, and we keep the list up to date.
    • Use #amm and consider creating a hashtag for your site or event to track your progress
    • If you have any questions, email Andrew
     

    During the event

    • Online events: Share important links like the AMM Missing Maps page and mapping user registration page in a common chat or online meeting lobby
    • Make sure to remind mappers which projects to work on. They are listed on this page. The Red Cross asked us for help with these projects, which are all good for beginners, and we keep the list up to date.
    • Remind mappers to square the corners of their buildings by clicking q on your keyboard after mapping it.
    • Have fun mapping!
     

    After event

    • Check the stats for your hashtag at the Missing Maps leaderboard page.
    • Send out a recap to the participants with stats, a thank you and whatever else you see fit.
    • Host another mapathon again soon!
